Mission

We want to make technology that's relevant to the actual person. The tonsorial arts has yet to be recognized and has yet to be truly tapped into by technology. Men do not often have services catered to them– there's services like Glamsquad, Zeel, Task Rabbit, and Wag, but none of these are relevant to the millennial man, especially college students. Men in university want a cheap yet quality haircut, it’s a necessity!

Finding a barber you trust wherever you may be is a difficult task that Google searches just can’t do, and Yelp reviews simply aren’t enough. Our goal is to become the largest grooming services platform – find a barber in your area, book for now or later, walk straight into the barber’s chair when you need to be there, or have the barber come to you for less than price of two ubers.

We wanted to make something that people really want to use and is accessible for and representative of all ethnicities. Our aims are not rooted in profit. Our partners pay at most 35 dollars a month for our software, brand development and exposure. Other companies charge upwards of 100 dollars for their barbershop booking software; We just want all our bros to eat (flex emoji)
